Swedish Objects

Description

Now on Steam, the original swedish armed forces experience for ArmA. Established in 2002 and still active today.

Swedish Forces Pack: Objects (SFP Objects) features buildings spanning from military barracks, radars, and coastal defences, to industrial and commercial facitilies, and civilian housing.

Designed to match the equipment present in the Swedish Forces Pack (SFP), these buildings are perfect for any nordic country setting, be it rural, urban, or military areas.

For example usage of SFP Objects, take a look at @sfp_sturkoe_island or @sfp_roso
